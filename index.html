Selected research

I work from dynamics and mathematical guarantees through simulation and implementation. These are the problems currently shaping my research direction.

EM-CR3BP · L1 HALO ORBITS

Astrodynamics · Current

Earth–Moon trajectory generation for autonomous operations

Developing a CR3BP trajectory-generation pipeline for L1 halo-orbit control datasets, including Lambert transfers and velocity-correction targets under Earth- and Moon-centered dynamics.

Affiliation
University of Auckland
Methods
CR3BP, Lambert targeting, numerical simulation

CONTROL BARRIER FUNCTION / h(x) ≥ 0

Safe autonomy

Safety-critical control for UAV-payload systems

Designed cascaded control barrier functions to enforce collision avoidance and state constraints during trajectory tracking, with investigations into higher-order and collision-cone CBFs.

Affiliation
Indian Institute of Science
Methods
CBFs, nonlinear control, constrained tracking
DOUBLE-GIMBAL VSCMG / ATTITUDE DYNAMICS

Spacecraft guidance & control

Robust spacecraft attitude control with VSCMG actuators

Building attitude-control simulations with double-gimbal variable-speed control moment gyroscopes, using sliding-mode and adaptive sliding-mode control for stabilization, tracking, and disturbance rejection.

Affiliation
IIT Kharagpur
Methods
VSCMG dynamics, SMC, adaptive control
FAULT MODE / MOTOR 02 OFFLINE

Quadcopter stabilization after single-motor failure

Developed a fault-tolerant control strategy and actuator-failure model that stabilizes a quadcopter after losing one motor. The project won Inter IIT Tech Meet 13.0.
