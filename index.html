Selected research and projects

Earth–Moon trajectory generation

University of Auckland, 2026

I am developing a CR3BP trajectory-generation pipeline for control datasets around Earth–Moon L1 halo orbits. The work also uses Earth- and Moon-centered Lambert transfers to generate velocity-correction targets.

Safety-critical control for a UAV-payload system

Indian Institute of Science, 2024

During my internship at the Formal Control and Autonomous Systems Lab, I implemented cascaded control barrier functions for collision avoidance and constraint satisfaction during trajectory tracking. I also studied higher-order and collision-cone CBF formulations.

Spacecraft attitude control using VSCMGs

IIT Kharagpur, 2025–present

I am building spacecraft attitude-control simulations that include double-gimbal variable-speed control moment gyroscope dynamics. I have implemented sliding-mode and adaptive sliding-mode controllers for stabilization, trajectory tracking, and disturbance rejection.

Single-motor-failure quadcopter control

ideaForge and IIT Kharagpur, 2024

Our team developed a fault-tolerant control strategy to stabilize a quadcopter after the loss of one motor. The project won Inter IIT Tech Meet 13.0.

Presentation

Unmanned rover for sample pick-and-place

ISRO Robotics Challenge, 2023–2024

I designed the rover chassis and rocker-bogie suspension and used static and dynamic simulations to study load capacity, gradeability, and terrain handling. Our team reached the national semi-finals.
